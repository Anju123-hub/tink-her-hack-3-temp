Food order

      1.Childhood(1-12 years)-Growth and development


    • Calcium and Vitamin D (for strong bones)

      • eg:leafy greens,Dairy

    • Iorn(for blood production)

      • eg:Egg,beans

    • Protein(for muscle growth)

      • eg:fish,chicken

    • Healthy fats(for brain development)

      • eg:nuts,avocado


      Avoid: Junk food,sugary snacks


      2.Teenage years(13-19 years)-muscle and energy boost


    • protein (for bone density)

      • eg:chicken,eggs

    • Iorn(for menstruation and oxygen)

      • eg:Red meat,spinach

    • Omega-3s(for brain and skin health)

      • eg:Salmon,Walnuts

    • B Vitamins(for energy and mood)

      • eg:eggs,bananas

      Avoid:excess alcohol,high-sugar foods


      3.Young adult(20-35 years)-muscle and metabolism maintenence


    • high-quality protein(for muscle repair)

      • eg:chicken,fish

    • complex carbs(for sustained energy)

      • eg:brown rise,quinoa,oats

    • Healthy fats(for heart and brain health)

      • eg:olive oil,avocado

    • antioxidants(for combat stress and health)

      • eg:berries,green tea


      Avoid:processed meats,excessive alcohol


      4.Middle age(35-50)-heart and metabolism


    • lean proteins(to maintain muscle mass)

      • eg:turkey,salmon

    • fiber(to support digestion and prevent)

      • eg:oats,beans

    • magnesium and zinc(for testosterone and immune health)

      • eg:fatty fish

      Avoid:

      excess sodium,refined carbs

      5.senior years(50-65 years)-testosterone and heart health


    • Calcium and vitamin D(for bone health)

      • eg:Dairy,juice

    • hydrating foods(to prevent dehydration)

      • eg:water-rich fruits

      Avoid:excess sugar,too much red meat


      6.Senior years(65+)


    • Protein-rich foods(to prevent muscle loss)

      • eg:egg,fish

    • healthy fats

      • eg:olive oil,avocado

      Avoid:excess sugar,high sodium proessed meals
